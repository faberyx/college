Membership

Gorilla Island Golf Club is one of premier parkland courses. As a former host venue to the Ladies Irish Open, the Seniors Irish Open and the Irish PGA Championship the club is steeped in history. We offer a wide variety of exciting golf membership packages, carefully designed to suit every golfer.
